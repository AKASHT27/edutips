DMIT

STRIKING ARIGHT BALANCE BETWEEN ABILITIES AND PASSION

A revolutionary technique, DMIT also referred to as Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test, is used to help the children and the parents identify their child’s inherent strengths and talent. DMIT enables in ascertaining the talent of an individual or ‘multiple intelligence’, which is based on dermatoglyphics and fingerprints. Fingerprint analysis is a scientific technique that helps unfold the individual’s potential. The system uses personality types, learning types, the potential of brain, intelligence and sensitivity index. The number of possible combinations gives an idea of the child’s future development. It is often seen in many scenarios that children select a career based on parental or peer pressure, and at times both. They end up with the burden of balancing the expectations of family or peers while selecting a course as well as their career, which ultimately leads them to not make the desired choice.

PSYCHOMETRIC TEST

FINDING A CAREER THAT INVOKES AND BOOSTS PRODUCTIVITY IN LONG RUN

Psychometric tests help teens and individuals identify acquired skills and talents inside them, thereby deciding which career path to take while they move forward in their studies. The test further helps individuals to make their life-changing decisions appropriately rather than just being swept off by the latest trends. Psychometric tests are the scientific ways used to explore a student’s attitude, skills, and interests to assess their personality. The purpose of conducting a psychometric test is to gauge the personality type, behavioural traits, and capacities of the students with the age of 14 and above. The psychometric test serves as great insight for companies to hire the right candidate. This is done through a meticulously drafted questionnaire that covers the Eight Multiple Intelligences. All of these when put together help to get an understanding of the individual’s aptitudes in various streams according to their interests.
